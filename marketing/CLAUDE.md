# Marketing CLAUDE.md

This folder contains marketing assets and video content for VOXAM, created with Remotion.

## VOXAM Brand Context

### Product Overview
VOXAM is an AI-powered educational examination platform that enables students to:
- Upload study materials (PDFs, textbooks)
- Automatically generate question papers
- Take interactive voice-based exams with AI proctoring
- Receive instant AI-driven feedback and corrections

### Target Audience
- College students preparing for exams
- Students who prefer voice-based learning over text
- Learners who want personalized study assistance
- Educational institutions looking for AI-assisted assessment

### Key Features to Highlight
1. **Voice-First Experience** - Natural conversation with AI examiner
2. **Smart Document Processing** - Upload any PDF, get instant question papers
3. **Real-time Feedback** - Know your mistakes immediately after answering
4. **Adaptive Learning** - LEARN mode for practice, EXAM mode for testing
5. **Comprehensive Reports** - Detailed correction reports with explanations

- Primary: `#6366f1` (Indigo-500) - Main brand color
- Secondary: `#8b5cf6` (Violet-500) - Accent color
- Background Dark: `#0f172a` (Slate-900)
- Background Light: `#f8fafc` (Slate-50)

- Error: `#ef4444` (Red-500)

### Typography
- Headlines: Inter (Bold/Semibold)
- Body: Inter (Regular/Medium)
- Code/Terminal: JetBrains Mono

### Visual Style
- Modern, clean, minimalist
- Dark mode preferred for videos
- Subtle gradients (indigo to violet)
- Smooth animations with easing
- Terminal/code aesthetics for tech appeal

## Video Formats

### Instagram Reels / TikTok
- Resolution: 1080x1920 (9:16 vertical)
- Duration: 15-60 seconds
- FPS: 30

### YouTube Shorts
- Resolution: 1080x1920 (9:16 vertical)
- Duration: up to 60 seconds
- FPS: 30

### YouTube Videos
- Resolution: 1920x1080 (16:9 horizontal)
- FPS: 30 or 60

### Landing Page Animations
- Resolution: varies (typically 1920x1080 or component-specific)
- Loop-friendly for backgrounds
- Lightweight for web performance

# VOXAM Product Features

## Core Value Proposition
"Turn your notes into real exam practice with voice-based AI that tests understanding, not memorization."

## How It Works (User Journey)

### 1. Upload
- Upload any study material (PDFs, textbooks, notes)
- Supports handwritten notes (OCR)
- No formatting required

### 2. Generate
- AI creates exam questions from your material
- Questions test deep understanding, not recall
- Different difficulty levels
- Various question types

### 3. Practice
- Voice-based exam practice
- Explain answers out loud
- AI asks follow-up questions
- Adaptive to your level

### 4. Feedback
- Instant feedback on every answer
- Know exactly where understanding breaks down
- Detailed correction reports
- Track progress over time

## Key Features

### Document Processing
- **Upload anything**: PDFs, images, handwritten notes
- **Smart extraction**: AI understands content structure
- **Chapter organization**: Automatically organized by topic

### Question Generation
- **Deep understanding questions**: Not just "what is X?"
- **Follow-up questions**: "Why?" "How?" "What if?"
- **Multiple formats**: Open-ended, scenario-based, application
- **Difficulty levels**: Easy, medium, hard

### Voice-Based Exams
- **Natural conversation**: Talk like you're explaining to a friend
- **Real-time processing**: Instant understanding of your answer
- **Adaptive follow-ups**: AI probes deeper based on your response
- **No typing needed**: Focus on thinking, not typing

### Two Modes
1. **LEARN Mode**
   - Hints available
   - Exploratory conversation
   - No time pressure
   - Great for initial study

2. **EXAM Mode**
   - No hints
   - Strict time limits
   - Simulates real exam pressure
   - Tests true readiness

### Feedback & Reports
- **Instant feedback**: Know immediately if you're right
- **Explanation of mistakes**: Understand WHY you were wrong
- **Correction reports**: Detailed breakdown per topic
- **Progress tracking**: See improvement over time

### Chat with Documents
- **Ask anything**: Query your documents naturally
- **RAG-powered**: Retrieves relevant content
- **Context-aware**: Remembers conversation history
- **Citation support**: Shows where info comes from

## Technical Differentiators

### Why Voice > Text
| Text Exams | VOXAM Voice |
|------------|-------------|
| Can copy-paste | Must explain in own words |
| Time to Google | Instant response required |
| Surface answers OK | Follow-ups expose gaps |
| Tests writing | Tests thinking |
| Can bluff | Can't hide confusion |

### AI Under the Hood
- **LiveKit**: Real-time voice streaming
- **LangGraph**: Intelligent conversation flow
- **Hybrid Search**: Vector + keyword for retrieval
- **Neo4j**: Graph-based document understanding
- **Multiple LLMs**: Best model for each task
